HITMAN 2

Hitman 2: Silent Assassin (abbreviated to "Silent Assassin" or "SA") is a stealth video game developed by IO Interactive and published by Eidos Interactive for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 2, Xbox, Nintendo GameCube. It is the second installment in the Hitman video game series and the sequel to Hitman: Codename 47. It was released on October 1st, 2002 in the United States of America. The player takes the place of a bald assassin named Agent 47 after he killed his creator, Dr. Otto Wolfgang Ort-Meyer and retires to a small church in Sicily.
